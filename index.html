Frame Rate Visualization

This page was created as a simple way to visualize the difference in common frame rates in video, film, games, and other visual content with motion. Hover over a track to see the slider move at the listed frame rate.

This test works best in the version 26 or newer of the desktop version of Google Chrome. Other browsers have trouble performing the animations smoothly. Performance is dependent on many factors, including your CPU, GPU, RAM, and browser stability. If you're having trouble, please see these tips for improving performance.
