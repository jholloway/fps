About This Tool

This page was created as a simple way to visualize the difference in common frame rates in video, film, games, and other visual content with motion. Hover over a track to see the slider move at the listed frame rate.

This test works best in version 26 or newer of the desktop version of Google Chrome. Other browsers have trouble performing the animations smoothly. This test will not work on touch devices. Performance is dependent on many factors. If you're having trouble, please see these tips for improving performance.
